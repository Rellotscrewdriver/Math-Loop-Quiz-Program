
	Math Loop Quiz Program
	
	case 1:
	2x1=?
	options 1 or 2
	if choose 2, right else choose 1, wrong.
	
	case 2:
	5x2=?
	options 10 or 8
	if choose 10, correct else choose 8, wrong.

	case 3:
	5+2=?
	options 10 or 7
	if choose 10, wrong else choose 7, correct.

	Number of correct answers= no. out of 3
	Number of incorrect answers= no. out of 3
